The Hallway

The house has a short hallway running east-west through the center of it, with doors along the sides and each end. You are familiar with the layout.

Billy's parent's room is down the hall on the left. The bathroom is at the end of the hall, and Billy's room is across from his parent's room.

Down the hall the other way is the dining room and the living room.

Right in front of you is the coat closet.

You can:

  • go north to the closet
  • go northeast to the master bedroom
  • go east to the bathroom
  • go southeast to Billy's room
  • go south to the foyer
  • go southwest to the dining room

or stop playing.
